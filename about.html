About Us

Atika Islamic High School (أتيكا الثانوية الإسلامية) was established to provide students with quality education while upholding strong Islamic values. Our mission is to nurture knowledgeable, responsible, and morally upright individuals who can contribute positively to society.

We offer a holistic learning environment that combines academic excellence with character development. Students are encouraged to engage in both scholastic and extracurricular activities, fostering teamwork, leadership, and creativity.

Our dedicated teachers and staff are committed to guiding students in their academic journey, spiritual growth, and personal development. At Atika Islamic High School, we believe that education is not just about grades, but about shaping well-rounded individuals prepared for the challenges of the modern world.

Message from the Principal

“Welcome to Atika Islamic High School. We are proud to provide a nurturing environment where every student can thrive. Our focus on knowledge, faith, and discipline ensures that students leave our school prepared for success in both worldly and spiritual matters.”
